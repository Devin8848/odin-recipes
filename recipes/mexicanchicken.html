Mexican Chicken Bowls with Cauliflower Rice

4 Serving with 400 Calories C:17 F:17 P:48

Ingredients

  • 1 head cauliflower
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 green bell pepper (chopped)
  • 1 can chopped tomatoes (1.5 cup chopped tomatoes)
  • 1 tsp ground onion
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 tsp crushed chili flakes
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • pinch of salt
  • pinch of black pepper
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • 1 cup wild rocket salad
  • 1 avocado

Steps for the Chicken

  1. Get a large pot, add chicken breasts to it, and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cover the chicken with around 1 inch of water.
  3. Bring the water to a boil and let it simmer for around 12 minutes. Remove the chicken from water and shred it using either fork, claws, or a food processor.

Steps for the Cauliflower rice

  1. Break apart cauliflower head. Wash, dry, and chop into smaller pieces.
  2. Pulse with a food processor until you’ve got cauliflower rice.
  3. Heat up a pan, add olive oil, chop green pepper and sauté them for a couple of minutes.
  4. Add cauliflower rice, chopped tomatoes, and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Mix in onion, garlic powder, cumin, paprika, crushed chili flakes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix as well as you can and let it simmer for 5 minutes.

Final Steps

  1. Serve into 4 containers
  2. Add the shredded chicken and the wold rocket between them
  3. Slice the avacado and split between the bowls
