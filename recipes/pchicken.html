Spicy Pineapple Chicken

3 Serving with 324 Calories C:29 F:10 P:31

This is a great low calorie high proein meal, and will still hit the cravings to eat takeout

Ingredients for the Maranade

  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 4 tbsp pineapple juice (from can)
  • 1 tbsp chilli sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger (grated)
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes

Ingredients for the Chicken

  • 500g chicken thighs, skinless & boneless (cubed)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 white onion (sliced)
  • 1 jalape√±o (sliced)
  • 400g can pineapple chunks (drained)
  • p1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp green onion (sliced)

Steps

  1. Drain the can of pineapples, leaving out the juice. Combine soy sauce, pineapple juice, chili sauce, garlic, ginger, and pepper flakes in a bowl.
  2. Cube the chicken thighs and add them to the marinade. Make sure all of it is covered, marinate in the fridge for 20 minutes or so.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add in the onion, jalapeno. Cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add in the chicken with the marinade. Afterward, turn up the heat and cook for 10-15 minutes until the chicken is fully cooked.
  5. Finally, add in pineapple and salt. Cook for another 3-5 minutes. Sprinkle with green onion before serving.

You can pair this with some form of rice for a great meal, or just eat by itself
